About Website

EngLearn is an English learning website that was developed with the aim of helping many people to learn English for free, anytime, and anywhere. EngLearn will help you understand the material more easily. In addition to the material, your understanding will be further improved with practice questions for each material provided. You will also be asked to login to be able to access this practice question. If you did not previously have an account on this website, you will be directed to registration. Of course, this website is equipped with a comment menu, where visitors can provide responses in the form of suggestions or criticisms about the website as well as questions about materials and questions that have not been understood. I hope this helps !

WHO WE ARE ?

The four of us are the developers of this EngLearn website with each jobdesk. Currently we are studying in college at the Information Technology Education Study Program, Surabaya State University, Indonesia.

WHAT WE DO ?

We developed this website, one of which is based on a web programming course assignment. The reason we chose the theme of Learning English is that there are still many people who have difficulty getting access to learning English. Of course, we provide this website for free.

OUR SERVICES

The services we include on this website are the availability of English language materials, examples of practice questions, and comment submission services. Of course the services we provide help you improve your English speaking skills.
